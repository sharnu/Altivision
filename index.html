FAQs

Questions, answered

Everything you'd want to know before enrolling. Still curious? Drop us a message.

Who can join SAP courses at Altivision?+
Anyone with a graduate degree — fresh graduates, working professionals from IT or non-IT backgrounds, and career switchers are all welcome. We map your background to the SAP module that fits best during your free counseling call.
Do you provide placement support?+
Yes. Our placement team helps with resume building, LinkedIn revamp, mock interviews, scenario rounds and direct referrals to our 100+ hiring partners across India and abroad.
Are classes online or offline?+
Both. You can attend live online sessions or visit our Lohegaon campus in Pune. Many students mix both based on their schedule. All sessions are recorded for revision.
Will I get SAP certification guidance?+
Yes. Each course includes structured certification preparation — mock tests, exam strategy, study material and registration assistance for official SAP global certifications.
Do freshers really get jobs after SAP training?+
Absolutely. A significant share of our alumni are fresh graduates now placed as associate SAP consultants. The key is the real-time project and interview prep — both included by default.
How long is each SAP course?+
Most modules run 3 to 5 months including a real-time project. Fast-track and weekend formats are available depending on the module. Your counselor will share an exact roadmap.
Do you provide SAP server access for practice?+
Yes, every learner gets dedicated SAP server access throughout the course duration so configurations, scripts and reports can be practiced hands-on.
