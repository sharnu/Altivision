FAQs

SAP support, answered

The questions IT and finance teams ask us most before signing an AMS contract.

Which SAP versions and modules do you support?+
We support SAP S/4HANA and SAP ECC, plus SAP SuccessFactors and SAP BTP. Functional coverage spans FICO, MM, SD, PP, EWM, PS and SuccessFactors, with technical support across ABAP, Fiori/UI5, SAP Basis/HANA and integration (CPI, PI/PO).
How are annual maintenance charges calculated?+
Annual support is priced as a fixed yearly maintenance contract based on the modules in scope, number of users, the SLA tier you need (support window and response times) and expected ticket volume. We share a tailored quote after a short landscape assessment.
Can you take over support from our current SAP partner?+
Yes. We run a structured transition with knowledge takeover (KT), documentation review and a parallel-run period before steady-state support begins, so there's no disruption to your operations.
Is support delivered onshore or offshore?+
Delivery is primarily India-based (remote) for cost efficiency, with onsite visits arranged for critical phases such as go-lives, upgrades and audits. Coverage windows range from 8×5 to 24×7 depending on the plan.
What SLAs do you offer?+
We work to priority-based SLAs (P1–P4) with defined response and resolution targets. P1 response ranges from 8 business hours on Essential to 1 hour on Enterprise, with monthly SLA reporting shared with your team.
How do you handle data security and confidentiality?+
Every engagement is covered by an NDA. We use role-based access aligned to least privilege, follow your change-control and data-handling policies, and don't export production data outside agreed boundaries.
